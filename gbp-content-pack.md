**Q5. Do you charge any fees from candidates?**
Absolutely not. Shiva Travel & Manpower Consultants follows a strict zero-fee policy for all candidates -- this is in line with Indian government regulations for overseas recruitment. Our placement fees are paid entirely by the foreign employer. If anyone claims to represent us and asks for money, please report it to our Nakodar office immediately.

### Visa Process

**Q6. How long does the overseas job placement process take?**
The timeline depends on the destination country and the type of visa, but most Gulf placements (Dubai, Saudi Arabia, Qatar) take 30 to 60 days from interview selection to departure. European work visa processing for countries like Romania and Poland can take 60 to 120 days due to additional embassy procedures. We keep candidates updated at every step from our Nakodar office.

**Q7. What documents do I need to apply for an overseas job?**
You will need a valid passport (with at least one year of validity), educational certificates, any trade or skill certificates, recent passport-sized photographs, and an Aadhaar card. For certain European positions we may also need a police clearance certificate and attested documents. Visit our office on Jalandhar Road, Nakodar, and our team will give you a personalized document checklist based on the job you are applying for.

**Q8. What is E-Migrate and why is it important?**
E-Migrate is a Government of India online system that protects Indian workers going abroad for employment. Every candidate placed through our agency is registered on the E-Migrate portal, which means your employment contract, employer details, and insurance are all verified and recorded by the government. This is one of the strongest safeguards you have as a worker, and our Nakodar team handles the entire E-Migrate registration for you.

**Q9. Is a medical test required before going abroad for work?**
Yes, most destination countries require a medical fitness certificate from a government-approved (GAMCA-registered) medical centre. For Gulf countries the test includes blood work, a chest X-ray, and a general physical examination. We guide all candidates from our Jalandhar-Nakodar region to the nearest approved medical centres and help coordinate the appointment.

**Q10. Who pays for the visa and travel costs?**
In all placements handled by Shiva Travel & Manpower Consultants, the visa cost and air ticket are paid by the employer as per Indian law and standard recruitment agreements. Candidates from Nakodar, Jalandhar, or anywhere in Punjab do not pay any visa or travel fee. We ensure complete transparency so you know exactly what the employer is providing before you depart.

### Countries

**Q11. Which countries do you send workers to?**
We recruit workers for the United Arab Emirates (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah), Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain, Kuwait, Romania, and Poland. Our strongest placement volumes are in the UAE and Saudi Arabia for construction, hospitality, and industrial trades, while our European placements focus on manufacturing and warehouse roles. Candidates from Punjab can visit our Nakodar office for the latest country-specific openings.

**Q12. What types of jobs are available in Dubai and the UAE?**
Dubai and the wider UAE offer opportunities for electricians, plumbers, welders, AC technicians, drivers, security guards, hospitality staff, and general labourers. Salaries typically range from AED 1,200 to AED 3,500 per month depending on the trade and experience level. We have strong employer relationships in Dubai and regularly conduct interviews at our Nakodar office or at our Jalandhar interview venues.

**Q13. What jobs do you have for Saudi Arabia?**
Saudi Arabia is one of our largest markets, with openings in construction, oil and gas, facilities management, and hospitality. Roles include carpenters, masons, steel fixers, heavy equipment operators, electricians, and cleaning staff, with salaries ranging from SAR 1,000 to SAR 3,000 plus accommodation and food. Our Nakodar team can share the latest Saudi demand list with you over WhatsApp or in person.

**Q14. Do you handle jobs in European countries?**
Yes, we are actively placing workers in Romania and Poland under legal European work visa categories. These positions are mostly in manufacturing, agriculture, food processing, and warehouse operations, with salaries significantly higher than Gulf placements. European work visas processed through our Nakodar office include full legal documentation, and we guide candidates from Punjab through every step of the embassy process.

**Q15. What is the process for Romania and Poland work visas?**
For Romania and Poland, the employer first obtains a work permit from their country's labour authority, after which we coordinate your visa appointment at the respective embassy in New Delhi. The process involves document attestation, a visa interview, and sometimes a trade-specific skill test. Our team in Nakodar handles the paperwork end to end, and we conduct a pre-departure orientation so you know what to expect when you land in Europe.

### Trust & Safety

**Q16. How can I verify that Shiva Travel & Manpower Consultants is a genuine agency?**
You can verify our license RA B-1794 on the Ministry of External Affairs website or the E-Migrate portal. We have been operating from our Nakodar office since 1998, and we welcome walk-in visits so you can see our office, meet our team, and check our licence certificate displayed on the wall. We also encourage you to read our Google reviews from real candidates placed across the Gulf and Europe.

**Q17. What happens if I face a problem after reaching the foreign country?**
Your welfare is our responsibility even after you leave India. We maintain regular contact with placed candidates and their employers, and if any issue arises -- whether it is a contract dispute, accommodation problem, or emergency -- you can call or WhatsApp our Nakodar office and we will intervene on your behalf. Because every candidate is registered on E-Migrate, the Indian Embassy in your destination country can also assist you.

**Q18. Do you have reviews from past candidates?**
Yes, we have reviews from hundreds of satisfied candidates on Google and social media. Many of our placements come through referrals from workers already abroad who recommend our Nakodar agency to their families and friends back in Punjab. We encourage every candidate to share their honest experience so new applicants from the Jalandhar area can make an informed decision.

### Specific

**Q19. Can you help me get a passport?**
While we are not a passport office, our team in Nakodar regularly assists candidates with the passport application process, including filling out the online form, scheduling appointments at the Jalandhar Passport Seva Kendra, and advising on the documents required. If you already have a passport, we will check its validity and advise whether a renewal is needed before your overseas placement begins.

**Q20. Do you provide any pre-departure training or orientation?**
Yes, every candidate placed through Shiva Travel & Manpower Consultants attends a pre-departure orientation session at our Nakodar office. This covers your employment contract terms, the laws of the destination country, cultural dos and don'ts, emergency contact numbers, and your rights as an overseas worker. For certain trades we also coordinate skill or trade testing to ensure you meet the employer's requirements before departure.
