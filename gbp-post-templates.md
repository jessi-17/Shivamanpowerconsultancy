2. **Photos:** Always add a real photo (office, team, clients, visa stamps — NOT stock)
3. **Time:** Post between 9 AM - 12 PM IST for best visibility
4. **Rotate:** After 12 weeks, start again with updated content
5. **Mention cities:** Include "Nakodar", "Jalandhar", "Punjab" in posts naturally
6. **Track:** Note which posts get the most views/clicks
